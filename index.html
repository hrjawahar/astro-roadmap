D1–D9 Life Pattern Analyzer

How this version works

Enter D1 and D9 manually. The app validates the charts, sends them to the analysis function, then returns rule-based insight and feedback for identity, wealth, marriage, career, restraint, foreign travel, and health sensitivity.

  • Lagna sign is mandatory for both charts.
  • Enter planets house-wise, comma separated: Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu.
  • Each planet should appear once in D1 and once in D9.
  • Mahadasha watch signals are shown in a separate tab.
